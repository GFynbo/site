Contract Database

PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

This is a web application that allows a business owner to have a personalized online contract for his customers to fill out and submit also allowing them to electronically sign said contracts. After the contract has been submitted the owner can then use multiple options on the database page to either send an email reminding them they have a payment due, print their contract out, or update their contact information. This web application uses HTML5, CSS, PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript.
